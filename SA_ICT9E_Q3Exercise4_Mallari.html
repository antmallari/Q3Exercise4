Hong Kong

When I visited Hong Kong in 2015, I was only 8 years old. My time in Hong Kong is something I'll always remember. There were a ton of places to visit, including Ocean Park, Disneyland, and many others. My favorite location in all of Hong Kong that we visited was the Ocean park. mainly due to the sheer number of rides and animals.

What to do in Hong Kong: Ocean Park, Hong Kong Disneyland, Ngong Ping 360
